Support

Need help with Appiness? Contact support or review the quick help topics below.

Contact

Email: innernoiseapp@gmail.com

Please include your device model, iOS or iPadOS version, Appiness version, and a short description of the issue.

Quick help

  • Restore purchases: open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Import sounds: use the import button to add audio files from your device or cloud storage.
  • Download sounds: use the Appiness online library to download selected sounds in MP3 or lossless quality.
  • Manage storage: remove sounds from the Sound Bank when you no longer need them.
  • Subscriptions: subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID settings.
